About us

About us

Oriental charm, enchanting landscapes, bewitching perfumes, sublime lights - Morocco is a source of constant inspiration for Zeina Yatim.

She created Rêve-Bienêtre ("Dream of Well-Being") in 2008, drawing on her sensitivity to women's authentic beauty rituals and traditional craftsmanship. The oriental dream is now accessible to every woman, if only for a bath.

The bath becomes a special moment, a moment filled with emotions, where the aromas of rose, orange blossom and ylang-ylang marry with luscious creams and rich oils. Zeina Yatim has created a range of products made of carefully selected, natural ingredients that are a true invitation to well-being.
