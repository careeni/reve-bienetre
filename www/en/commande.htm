Right of withdrawal

Pursuant to article L121-16 of the French Consumer Code, the client shall have a period of seven working days as of delivery of his order to return the goods to SARL AZ Import Export for exchange or reimbursement, without incurring any additional costs, except for return postage fees.

Protection of personal data

Your personal data is required to process your orders. In compliance with French "information technology and civil liberties" law no. 78-16, dated January 6, 1978, which pertains to information technology, databases and civil liberties, you have the right to access, consult, modify and delete the data you have communicated to us, by contacting SARL AZ Import Export customer service.

Delivery problems

In the case of damaged products or a delivery error, please contact us.

- If the product was damaged during shipment, contact us on the same day the delivery arrives or, at the latest, on the first working day after delivery via email to : contact@reve-bienetre.com to set a new delivery date.
- If the merchandise is not what you ordered, or if a product is missing, contact us on the same day the delivery arrives or, at the latest, on the first working day after delivery and we will correct the error as soon as possible.
