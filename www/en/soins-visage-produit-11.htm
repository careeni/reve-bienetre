Face care

This product rich in vitamins A, D, E and K, nourishes, protects and softens skin. Replenished with fatty acids, skin remains supple and more resilient to aging and external stress factors. This treatment also prevents hair from losing its luster and strengthens nails.

Thanks to a high concentration of silica, calcium, iron, potassium and magnesium, this mask boasts powerful mineral-replenishing properties. Astringent, purifying, sebum-regulating, regenerating and revitalizing. It restores balance to oily skin and fights against blemishes (pimples, blackheads...). Recommended for all skin types, including oily skin.

Available in a professional salon format (500 ml)

Ingredients: AQUA , ARGILA, BENTONITE , ANTHEMIS NOBILIS FLOWER WATER*, OLEA EUROPEA FRUIT OIL*, GLYCERYL STEARATE, SUCROSE STEARATE , BENZYL ALCOHOL , GLYCERIN, TOCOPHEROL, HELIANTHUS ANNUUS SEED OIL, PARFUM, SODIUM BENZOATE, CITRIC ACID, POTASSIUM SORBATE, ROSMARINUS OFFICINALIS EXTRACT*, SALVIA OFFICINALIS EXTRACT*.

Made in France

Apply a thick and even layer (2 to 3mm) of clay to healthy, dry face and neck with fingertips, avoiding the eye contour. Leave on for 10 minutes maximum. Moisten the clay if dry. Rinse off with lukewarm water.

For improved moisture replenishment, apply a moisturizing cream to the face after removing the revitalizing mask. Frequency of use: May be applied as often as needed; ideally one to two times a week.
