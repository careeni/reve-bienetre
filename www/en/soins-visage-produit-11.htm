Face

Rich in vitamins A, D, E and K, this mask nourishes, protects and softens the skin.
The skin full of fatty acids remains supple and prevents the ageing signs and outside attacks. Avoid the tarnishing of the hair, and strengthens the nails. Its strong content in silica, calcium, iron, potassium and magnesium gives the mask a remineralizing power.

Astringent, purifying, regenerating and revitalizing, the mask rebalances oily skin and reduces skin problems (buttons, blackheads).
For all types of skins, even the oiliest.

Available in large format (500 ml)

Ingredients: AQUA , ARGILA, BENTONITE , ANTHEMIS NOBILIS FLOWER WATER*, OLEA EUROPEA FRUIT OIL*, GLYCERYL STEARATE, SUCROSE STEARATE , BENZYL ALCOHOL , GLYCERIN, TOCOPHEROL, HELIANTHUS ANNUUS SEED OIL, PARFUM, SODIUM BENZOATE, CITRIC ACID, POTASSIUM SORBATE, ROSMARINUS OFFICINALIS EXTRACT*, SALVIA OFFICINALIS EXTRACT*.(*)Organically grown

Made in France

Apply generously with fingers on dry healthy skin, avoiding the eye area, until you obtain a uniform thick layer (2/3mm) on your face and your neck. Leave it for 10 minutes max.
Re-moisten the clay if it dries. Rinse with warm water.

For a better hydration of the skin, apply a moisturizing cream on your face after this revitalizing mask.

Frequency of use : so often as you wish it, ideally one or twice a week.
Alcohol, phenoxyethanol and paraben free.
