Dry oil with Argan & Orange Blossom Honey - 50ml or 100ml

Our dry oil envelops skin and hair in silky softness, while offering a unique sensory experience, thanks to the sweet scent of Orange Blossom Honey

This genuine cocktail of nutrients is formulated with Argan and Sunflower Oils, boasting toning, protective and firming properties. Immediately upon application, it delivers an intense sensation of moisture and is ideal for dehydrated and dull skin.

Rich in Omega 9, Omega 6 and Vitamin E.

Ingredients : CAPRYLIC/CAPRIC TRIGLYCERIDE, ISOPROPYL PALMITATE, HELIANTHUS ANNUUS SEED OIL*, ARGANIA SPINOSA KERNEL OIL*, PARFUM, TOCOPHEROL, LIMONENE, GERANIOL.

Made in France.

For optimal absorption, it is best to use after a shower or bath.
Place a dab of oil in the palm of your hand. Rub hands together and spread the oil over your entire body, from feet to neck, using circular strokes. May also be used for massage.

Frequency of use : For daily application.

Storage: Store at room temperature, away from direct sunlight.

Please note: Avoid contact with the eyes. Do not ingest.
